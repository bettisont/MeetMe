How It Works

  1. Enter two or more London postcodes.
  2. Select the type of activity you're interested in (e.g., pub, cafe, park).
  3. Get a list of suggested activities with equal travel times for each person.

Why MeetMe?

  • Save time by finding a central meeting point.
  • Discover new places to meet and socialize.
  • Make planning group activities easy and convenient.
